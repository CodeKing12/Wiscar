Annual Women Convention 2022

We are delighted to announce our 2021 WISCAR Annual Conference theme: “Playing Big; Time to take our Place”. This year’s conference will showcase leading women, playing big in various fields, from social innovation, to politics, to entrepreneurship and corporate life, driving change and transformation. More than a talk shop, the conference generates useful recommendations to drive the required legal and policy changes for growth and national development. With a variety of events including virtual mentoring roundtables, youth panels, and keynote panel, there will be opportunities for delegates to interact and network with over 30 world class speakers, policy makers, global shapers, thought leaders, with the grand finale being the conferment of the Distinguished WISCAR Award on an innovative and visionary leader. Together we will distill concrete actions required to take bold actions play big and take our place in moving our nation forward.
