Benefits of becoming a member

Paid WISCAR membership entitles members to exclusive discounts and first information on the following;

  • All WISCAR Events
  • Free use of WISCAR Board room subject to availability and discount on the training room at the WISCAR Secretariat
  • WISCAR Annual Conference participation fee
  • Consideration of mutually beneficial partnership opportunities
  • Preferential Selection for WISCAR Mentoring Programmes and other similar opportunities
  • First to receive information about mentorship and other WISCAR opportunities before the general public
  • Opportunity to advertise products and services in our brochures and newsletters at applicable discounted rates
  • Inclusion in WISCAR WhatsApp Closed Community
